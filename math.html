Scenario 3: Acquiring Loans

Jake takes a ₱50,000 loan at 5% annual interest, to be repaid in 5 equal yearly installments.

Financial Mathematics Analysis using Geometric Sequences

Problem Setup & Given Information

Loan Details

  • Principal (P): ₱50,000
  • Annual Interest Rate (r): 5% = 0.05
  • Loan Term (n): 5 years
  • Payment Type: Equal annual installments

What We Need to Find

  • Annual payment amount (A)
  • Total amount paid over 5 years
  • Total interest paid
  • Amortization schedule

Mathematical Modeling: Loan Payment Formula

Present Value of Annuity Formula

A = P × [r(1+r)ⁿ] / [(1+r)ⁿ - 1]

Where:

  • A = Annual payment amount
  • P = Principal loan amount (₱50,000)
  • r = Annual interest rate (0.05)
  • n = Number of payments (5)

Step-by-Step Calculation

Step 1: Calculate (1+r)ⁿ

(1 + 0.05)⁵ = (1.05)⁵ = 1.2762815625

Step 2: Calculate the numerator

r × (1+r)ⁿ = 0.05 × 1.2762815625 = 0.0638140781

Step 3: Calculate the denominator

(1+r)ⁿ - 1 = 1.2762815625 - 1 = 0.2762815625

Step 4: Final calculation

A = ₱50,000 × (0.0638140781 ÷ 0.2762815625)

A = ₱50,000 × 0.230975

Annual Payment = ₱11,548.74
